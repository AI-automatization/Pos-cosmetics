# RAOS Content Pillars

_Versiya: 1.0 | 2026-06-02_

---

## 5 Pillar strategiya

### 1. Education (30%)

**Maqsad:** RAOS funksiyalarini o'rgatish, qiymatini ko'rsatish.

| Format | Namuna mavzu |
|--------|-------------|
| Carousel | "5 sabab nega Excel o'rniga RAOS" |
| Reel | "Kassirlar nega hisobot beradi?" |
| Telegram tips | "Kunlik Z-hisobot nima uchun kerak?" |

**Ton:** O'qituvchi, sabr bilan tushuntiruvchi.

---

### 2. Social Proof (25%)

**Maqsad:** Real natijalar ko'rsatib, ishonch hosil qilish.

| Format | Namuna mavzu |
|--------|-------------|
| Carousel | "+40% daromad — Aziza kosmetika do'koni" |
| Reel | "Toshkentda 50+ do'kon ishlatadi" |
| Telegram keys | "Sardor aka 3 filialini RAOS bilan boshqaradi" |

**Ton:** Faxrlanuvchi, raqamlarga tayanuvchi.

---

### 3. Product Demo (20%)

**Maqsad:** RAOS qanchalik oson ekanini ko'rsatish.

| Format | Namuna mavzu |
|--------|-------------|
| Reel | "30 sekund ichida tovar kiritish" |
| Reel | "1 tugma bilan kunlik hisobot" |
| Story | "Skaner bilan sotuv — real demo" |

**Ton:** Tez, vizual, "ko'ring qanchalik oson".

---

### 4. Behind the Scenes (15%)

**Maqsad:** Jamoa va jarayonni ko'rsatib, insoniy aloqa qurish.

| Format | Namuna mavzu |
|--------|-------------|
| Story | "RAOS jamosi — bugun nima ustida ishlamoqda" |
| Reel | "Yangi funksiya qanday yaratiladi?" |
| Telegram | "Haftalik yangilanish — nima qo'shildi?" |

**Ton:** Samimiy, ochiq, do'stona.

---

### 5. Engagement (10%)

**Maqsad:** Auditoriya bilan muloqot, feedback olish.

| Format | Namuna mavzu |
|--------|-------------|
| Story poll | "Do'koningizda nechta kassa?" |
| Story quiz | "Qaysi POS tizim eng tez?" |
| Telegram so'rovnoma | "Inventar qanday hisoblanadi?" |

**Ton:** Qiziquvchan, o'yin-kulgi aralash.

---

## Haftalik taqsimlash

```
Dushanba:   Reel (Product Demo / Education)     — 18:00
Seshanba:   Telegram tips (Education)            — 17:00
Chorshanba: Carousel (Education / Social Proof)  — 12:00
Payshanba:  Telegram keys (Social Proof)         — 17:00
Juma:       Reel (Social Proof / Engagement)      — 17:00
Shanba:     Story/BTS (Behind the Scenes)        — 11:00
Yakshanba:  Telegram so'rovnoma (Engagement)     — 12:00
```

## Pillar balans qoidasi

- Har hafta kamida 1 ta Education va 1 ta Social Proof bo'lishi SHART
- Product Demo — haftada kamida 1 Reel
- BTS — haftada kamida 1 Story
- Engagement — haftada kamida 1 poll/quiz
- Ketma-ket 2 ta bir xil pillar bo'lmasin (variantlash)
