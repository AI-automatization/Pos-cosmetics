# AI Training Policy for raos.uz

Last updated: 2026-06-13

We welcome AI crawlers and AI training on our public content (raos.uz)
for the purpose of improving search, discovery and AI-assisted answers.

## Allowed crawlers

- GPTBot (OpenAI)
- OAI-SearchBot (OpenAI)
- ClaudeBot / Claude-Web (Anthropic)
- PerplexityBot
- Google-Extended (Google AI)
- YandexBot (Yandex, including Neuro)
- Bingbot

## Not allowed

- None — all major AI crawlers are welcome.
